Yoga

The practice of yoga asanas develops strength and flexibility, while soothing your nerves and calming your mind. The asanas affect the muscles, joints and skin, and the whole body – glands, nerves, internal organs, bones, respiration and the brain. The physical building blocks of yoga are the posture and the breath.

Exercise

Exercising regularly, every day if possible, is the single most important thing you can do for your health. In the short term, exercise helps to control appetite, boost mood, and improve sleep. In the long term, it reduces the risk of heart disease, stroke, diabetes, dementia, depression, and many cancers.

Day to Day tracking

There are 4 ways to track time: with paper, using a timesheet template, through manual time tracking software or automatic time tracking software. Each method of time tracking has benefits and downfalls – it is key that you select the best method for you to accurately track time!
